High Effort

High Effort

Built an anomaly and trend detection system that continuously monitors feedback data to flag statistically significant shifts, both steady upward trends and sudden spikes. The model accounts for seasonality, baseline variance, and natural noise to reduce false alarms. Identifies patterns between response data and user attributes.

High Effort

Interview Copilot

Real-time transcription and next question recommendations to level up user interviews.

High Effort

The AI copilot listens to interview audio from any tool, transcribes it in real time, and suggests strong follow-up questions based on what the participant just said. The goal is to help teams run better interviews, especially PMs and founders without formal research training. It reduces the gap between hearing something interesting and asking the right next question. Under the hood, I use speech-to-text with diarization and translation, then LLM workflows that generate targeted probes and clarifying questions on the fly. The result is more consistent interviews and better insights without a dedicated researcher on every call. Was in staging environment only.

Semantic Chunking

Chunks long texts based on similar meaning rather than hard constraints.

Medium Effort

Built a semantic chunking pipeline that segments long-form feedback and transcripts based on meaning rather than arbitrary character or sentence limits. Using embedding similarity, the system detects natural topic boundaries so each chunk contains a coherent unit of thought. This prevents fragmented context (e.g., splitting a complaint mid-explanation) and improves downstream clustering, search, and RAG retrieval quality.
